Jiayi Liu(刘佳宜)

I am an associate professor, interested in Artificial intelligence in robotic disassembly , affiliated with the Wuhan University of Technology .

I have been invited to deliver presentations at numerous domestic and international academic conferences and have received paper awards from three international conferences, including the 2022 IEEE CASE. I serve as a peer review expert for the National Natural Science Foundation of China and as a thesis evaluation expert for the Ministry of Education. I am also a standing committee member of the first Digital Twin Young Scholars Committee (China) and have held roles as co-chair or committee member for five international symposiums. Furthermore, I serve as a reviewer for several top-tier international journals, including IEEE Transactions on Industrial Informatics and IEEE Transactions on Automation Science and Engineering.

Robotic disassembly: Perception methods, modelling and decision-making methods
Artificial intelligence: Deep learning, reinforcement learning, large language model
